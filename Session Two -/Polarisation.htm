Polarisation

Thursday, 20 September 2018

11:26 AM

The direction of polarisation of each wave is defined to be the direction the electric field is vibrating

 

A wave is linearly polarised if the resultant electric field E always vibrates in the same direction.

The plane formed by E and the direction of propagation is called the plane of polarisation.
